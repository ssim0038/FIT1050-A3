Sharing our passion for Asian cooking!

We're a family-run store passionate about sharing our love for Asian cooking, and celebrating our community's cultures through food! Whether you're cooking up mum's recipe, or trying a brand new dish, we have you covered!

We pride ourselves on our great prices, and our range - our customers often say if you can’t find it in Hong Kong, you can’t find it in Melbourne!

Visit us in Clayton and walk through our endless aisles packed with specialty Asian groceries from across Japan, Korea, China, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Vietnam, Thailand, Cambodia, Philippines, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, and more!
